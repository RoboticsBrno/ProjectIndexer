Semafor

Traffic light gadget for outdoor games designed for Helceletka.cz.

Hardware

Board is based on ESP8266 microcontroller and large LEDs. It is powered by our power bank and can be managed via WiFi from the user's phone.

Software

The software is written in PlatformIO and is based on the Arduino framework.
